My Hometown

I'm from a city in Brazil named Aracaju and it's located in Sergipe state, at NorthEast. It's not a big town but it's not exactly small, we have a lot of restaurants, beachs, bars (that me call "botecos"). We also have one of the best foods in the world! Some like cuscuz, caranguejo, pastel, feijoada, açaí, acarajé, amendoim and so many others! The city is called this way in honor of a bird called Arara and a fruit call Caju.
